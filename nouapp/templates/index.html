ABOUT NOU e-GYAN PORTAL

Nalanda Open University welcomes all our students, faculties & study centres to the very new & innovative online study portal NOU e-Gyan. The portal is a unique initiative by Hon'ble Vice-Chancellor Prof. (Dr.) K C Sinha, which aims at providing online study material to the students of NOU.

The portal is accessible from anywhere in the world with proper authorization. It is fully secure and available 24x7 for authorized users. It helps resolve academic issues such as E-content Distribution, Delivery, Tracking, Assessment, Progress Monitoring & Control for all stakeholders including students, teachers, study centre administrators, and NOU admins at a distance.
